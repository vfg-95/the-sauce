Egg Fried Rice

10   10m

  • 250g Long Grain/Jasmine Rice
  • 3 Eggs
  • Spring Onions
  • Soy Sauce
  • Vegetable Oil

This one is super cheap and easy for you guys, just remember you need to cook the rice and let it cool down in the fridge before you make it. Feel free to add extra veg or even meat, but we're keeping it veggie and keeping it simple. As this one is so quick we recommend just cooking it fresh each time you want it, the taste is worth it!

Step 1

Add 1 Tbsp of vegetable oil to a wok/frying pan and put it on a medium to high heat. Crack your eggs into a mixing bowl and add a dash of soy sauce, then whisk them up.

Step 2

Add the eggs to the pan and leave to cook. Start to slice your spring onions, keeping an eye on the egg.

Step 3

Once the egg is 3/4 cooked add another tablespoon of vegetable oil, then add your pre-cooked cold rice and give it a good mix together.

Step 4

Make sure the rice has heated up properly then add 2 Tbsp of soy sauce to the pan, leaving it for 10/15 seconds before you mix it into the egg and rice.

Step 5

Turn off the heat and stir in the sliced spring onions. That's it, you're done! Serve and enjoy.
