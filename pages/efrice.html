Egg Fried Rice

10   10m

  • 250g Long Grain/Jasmine Rice
  • 3 Eggs
  • Spring Onions
  • Soy Sauce
  • Vegatable Oil

This one is super cheap and easy for you guys, just remember you need to cook the rice and let it cool down in the fridge before you make it. Feel free to add extra veg or even meat, but we're keeping it veggie and keeping it simple. As this one is so quick we recommend just cooking it fresh each time you want it, the taste is worth it!
