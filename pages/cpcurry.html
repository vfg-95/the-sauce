Chickpea Curry

10   1h

  • 500g Basmati Rice/Microwave Coconut Rice
  • 2 x 400g Tins Chickpeas
  • 2 x 400g Tins Tomatoes
  • 2 x 400g Tins Coconut Milk (Only 1 Required If Using Microwave Rice)
  • 1 x Pack Of Spinach (Optional)
  • 1 x Pack of Chillies
  • Garlic
  • Ginger (Thumb Sized)
  • Curry Powder
  • Garam Masala
  • Tumeric
  • Ground Corriander
  • Mustard Seeds
  • Corrinder (Optional)
  • Lime (Optional)

Our longest recipe, but a great one for storing in the fridge or freezer once cooked and saving time down the line. If you intend on doing this then up the quantities of chickpeas & tinned tomatoes. You'll be be left with easily 5+ portions that you can bang straight in the microwave. There's a lot of ingredients however you can get all this for less than Â£15, if you're tight for cash then skip the optional ones. The same applies to the spices - this will taste good with just curry powder & tumeric.
