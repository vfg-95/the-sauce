Sweet Potato Wedges

4   30m-1hr

  • Sweet Potatoes
  • Olive Oil
  • Salt & Pepper

It really doesn't get any easier or cheaper than this. 1 Serving is equal to 2 medium/large potatoes. You can use any kind of cooking oil but olive oil does work best. Feel free to add your own spices, cajun always works very well.
